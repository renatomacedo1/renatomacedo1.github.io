QR Code reader for the visual impaired

Java desktop application capable of reading and generating QR Codes.

Android application capable of reading QR Codes and transform the texto to audible speech.
-Received information should be structured or not.
-100% offline
Research the optimization of QR Code with text compression.

Technologies
- ZXING, library to read and generate QR Code.
- JSON for information structure.
- “Text-to-Speech” – Android native
- Adaptive Huffman
