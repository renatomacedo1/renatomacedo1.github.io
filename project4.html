Subject reader for visually impaired - Iphone

Project designed on iOS to help blind people read newspaper, magazine titles by simply pointing from camera to text.

Application prepared for the needs of any user who needs to obtain narrated information from a textual source. In detail, the application:
- Selects the title text of the identified and separated text
- Narrate selected text

Technologies
- iOS, Vision Framework, Tesseract, Native iOS TTS
